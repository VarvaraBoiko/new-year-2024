Santa Claus, also known as Saint Nicholas, Kris Kringle, or Father Christmas, is a legendary figure who is said to bring gifts to well-behaved children on Christmas Eve. The modern depiction of Santa Claus is based on traditions associated with the Dutch figure Sinterklaas, as well as other influences from European folklore. He is typically portrayed as a jolly, rotund man with a white beard and a red suit. Santa's home is believed to be the North Pole, where he lives with his team of reindeer and industrious elves who help him prepare for the holiday season. The image of Santa Claus has become a central figure in Christmas celebrations around the world.
